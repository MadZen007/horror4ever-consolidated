$5000 Trivia Contest - FAQ & Rules

Everything you need to know about the Horror4Ever Trivia Contest

General Contest Information

What is the prize for winning?

$5000

How much does it cost to enter the contest?

$50

When does the contest start and end?

Starting 12:01am Oct. 1st - Ends 11:59pm Oct. 30th

How do I enter the contest?

Fill out the sign up form, and pay the entry fee, you will get a confirmation email showing you are officially enrolled

Where can I find the contest rules and terms & conditions?

Look further down this page for those sections.

Who is eligible to enter the contest?

Any USA residents who are over 18

Is this contest open internationally?

No

How will the winner be determined?

Highest score based on correct answers and speed

How will I be notified if I win?

Email, phone, website announcement. Winner will be sent a secure form to get direct deposit info. Winner has 7 days to reply

When will the winner be announced?

The website will automatically show the winner at 12:01am Oct. 31st

How will the prize be awarded?

Direct deposit

Contest Gameplay

How does the contest work?

500 questions, 10 seconds, scoring, highest score wins!

What kind of horror trivia will be asked?

All questions will be based on Horror movies

How difficult are the questions?

Varying difficulty levels

Can I pause or stop the contest once I've started?

No. One shot

What happens if I run out of time on a question?

Zero points for that question

Are there any breaks during the contest?

No, it is one shot, but the player can do it anytime within 30 days

What if I experience technical difficulties during the contest?

To be fair, we cannot do any retakes. We recommend you play on a PC when you are confident you have a solid connection, and no distractions

How is scoring calculated?

Faster answer = more points

Where can I see the leaderboard and how often is it updated?

Weekly updates on the website

Will I be able to see which questions I answered correctly or incorrectly?

After you answer a question, you will see the correct answer before you click a button to move onto the next question

What happens if there is a tie for the highest score?

Because it is 500 questions with 10 points possible for each one, based on speed....the odds of a tie are very slim, BUT if there is a tie for 1st place, those players will be notified and there will be another trivia quiz to determine the top score.

Technical Issues & Support

What if I have trouble registering for the contest?

Click on our support button, and the contest lasts all month, so you have time to get in

What if I have trouble making a payment?

Click on our support button

Legal & Ethical

Is my personal information safe?

We will be using secure forms to gather information

Is the payment process secure?

Payments are processed through Ko-fi, a well-known and secure third-party payment platform that handles millions of transactions worldwide.
