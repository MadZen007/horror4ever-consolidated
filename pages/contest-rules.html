Paid Entry: To enter the contest, visit Horror4ever.com and follow the instructions to register and pay the $50 entry fee.

Limit one (1) entry per person

3. Contest Period

The contest begins at 12:01am on October 1st, 2025, and ends at 11:59pm on October 30th, 2025. All entries must be received during the Contest Period.

4. How to Play and Win

Participants will answer 500 horror trivia questions of varying difficulty.

Each question is worth a maximum of 10 points.

Points are awarded based on speed. The point value decreases by 1 point for each second taken to answer a question.

The participant with the highest total score at the end of the Contest Period will be declared the winner.

Tie-Breaker: In the unlikely event of a tie, a new set of trivia questions will be sent to the tied participants. The participant with the highest score on the new set of questions will be declared the winner.

6. Winner Selection and Notification

The potential winner will be determined based on the highest score at the end of the Contest Period.

The winner will be notified by email or phone within 7 days of the end of the Contest Period., but the website will show the winner automatically at 12:01am October 31st, 2025

The winner will be required to sign and return an Affidavit of Eligibility, a Liability Waiver, and a Publicity Release (where permitted by law) within 7 days of notification.

If the winner fails to return the required documents within the specified time period, or if the prize notification is returned as undeliverable, the prize may be forfeited, and an alternate winner may be selected based on next highest score.

7. Skill-Based Contest

This is a skill-based contest, and the winner will be determined based on their knowledge of horror trivia and their ability to answer questions quickly and accurately. Chance plays no part in determining the winner.
